---

## Story

As a developer integrating the Fusion Framework,
I want to open the app-react cookbook and see a correctly rendered landing page,
so that I can confirm the framework initialises and mounts a React component without errors.

## Scenarios

- [ ] Given I open the application, When the page loads, Then I see a heading that reads "Hello Fusion" with a rocket and sunglasses emoji
- [ ] Given I open the application, When the page loads, Then the app has a light grey background
- [ ] Given I open the application, When the page loads, Then no error messages are visible

## Non-goals

- Authentication flows, responsiveness, or HTTP requests
